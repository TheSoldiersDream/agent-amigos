# OpenWork Workflow Template: Research & Synthesis

## Purpose

Collect, compare, and summarize information to guide a decision or implementation.

## When to Use

- Evaluating libraries or approaches
- Assessing trade-offs
- Preparing implementation recommendations

## Inputs

- Research question
- Constraints (budget, time, compliance)
- Required sources or benchmarks

## Outputs

- Summary of findings
- Comparison matrix
- Recommendation and rationale
- Next steps

## Suggested Session Prompt

"Research the options, summarize findings, and provide a clear recommendation with trade-offs."

## Workflow Steps (Todos)

1. Clarify the decision criteria and constraints.
2. Identify credible sources and gather data.
3. Compare options using a consistent rubric.
4. Highlight risks, gaps, and unknowns.
5. Recommend the best-fit option.
6. Provide implementation notes and next steps.

## Permissions

- Ask before using external web sources or paid APIs.

## Notes

- Keep summaries concise and actionable.
