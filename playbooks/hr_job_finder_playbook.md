# HR Job-Finder Playbook (Amigos Broker Model)

## Goal

Act as a recruitment middleman: find employers with open roles, source candidates, screen, and deliver a shortlist. Earn a placement fee or retainer + placement fee.

## Target Roles (start simple)

- Remote: Virtual Assistant, Customer Support, Appointment Setter
- Local PH: Hospitality staff, retail, basic admin

## Core Offer (1 sentence)

"We deliver 3–5 qualified candidates within 7–10 days, handle screening and scheduling, and only charge on placement."

## Fees (choose 1 model)

1. Per-hire placement: ₱12,000–₱25,000 per hire (local) or 10–15% of first month salary
2. Retainer + lower placement: ₱5,000–₱10,000/month + ₱8,000–₱15,000 per hire
3. Volume: ₱3,000–₱6,000 per hire (for 5+ hires/month)

## Workflow

1. Client intake (role, pay, requirements, start date)
2. Sourcing (job boards, FB groups, referrals)
3. Screening (short call + checklist)
4. Shortlist delivery (3–5 candidates)
5. Interview scheduling
6. Placement confirmation + invoice

## KPIs

- Leads contacted/week
- Client replies
- Jobs signed
- Candidates sourced
- Interviews scheduled
- Placements

## Tools to use in Amigos

- Outreach scripts (email/FB)
- Intake form
- Screening checklist
- Pipeline tracker CSV
